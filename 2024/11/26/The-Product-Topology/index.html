By the way, we prefer the product topology to the box topology. The answer will appear as we continue our study of topology. We shall find that a number of important theorems about finite products will also hold for arbitary products if we use the product topology, but not if we use the box topology. As a result, the product topology is extremely important in mathematics. The box topology is not so important; we shall use it primarily for constructing counterexamples, Therefore, we make the following convention:
